Arthur Dent Tribute Page

Here we will talk about the wacky and wild life of space explorer Arthur Dent!

WARNING! There will be spoilers for the hitchhiker's guide to the galaxy book series below so read at your own peril!

Pre-Space Adventures

There isn't a whole lot to say about Arthur Dent prior to his spacefaring adventures, just a pretty normal guy who worked in radio and lived in a rather unremarkable house, that was all soon to change though!

Space Adventures

Oh where to begin? I suppose the destruction planet earth at the beginning of book 1 is a good place to start! All for a space-highway if you can believe it! Anyway following this tragic event Arthur's journey through space begins alongside his good friend, Ford Prefect. Without going into too much detail, I will say Arthur does various fun things such as:

  • Learning to fly!
  • Teaching a vending machine about tea!
  • Meeting a guy with two heads!
  • Killing the same person through many different re-incarnations until they are driven mad!
  • And much more!
